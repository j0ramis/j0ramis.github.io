Welcome to the Bayou

Welcome to Jolly Gator Hot Sauce Co., where we craft small-batch, swamp-inspired artisan hot sauces, fiery dry rubs, and vibrant barbecue essentials designed to give your backyard grill-outs a clean, memorable kick.

We blend freshly harvested local peppers with authentic southern ingredients to create depth of flavor that complements chicken, ribs, seafood, and structural tailgating spreads alike.

Featured Spice Levels

Mild Bayou Kick

A smooth, tangy blend of aged cayenne, roasted garlic, and local honey. Perfect for everyday splashing without overwhelming your palate.

Classic Smoked Alligator

Our flag-bearer. Medium-heat jalapeños oak-smoked over open pits, mixed with apple cider vinegar and signature swamp aromatics.

Swamp Inferno

Pure heat. A bold combination of blistering ghost peppers and red habaneros balanced with dynamic citrus notes. Approach with caution.

Our Story

Founded deep within the coastal lowlands, Jolly Gator started as a backyard passion project driven by engineering structural barbecue recipes that don't take shortcuts. We believe great sauce balances clean acidity with sustained heat infrastructure.

Every single bottle is filled, labeled, and certified by hand using sustainable food production guidelines and transparent manufacturing channels.
